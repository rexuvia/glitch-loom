
## ModelShow Run: Odd numbers without 'e'
- Sky ran `/mdls` with "List 6 odd numbers that don't contain the letter 'e'".
- Models used: pro, deepseek, gpt5, grok, kimi, and sonnet46 (substituted for opus4 which failed).
- Interestingly, most models caught the trick (that in English, one, three, five, seven, nine, eleven all have 'e') but some just wrote digits. Kimi wrote numbers like "101 (one hundred one)". GPT-5 and Deepseek (the last one) wrote digits to avoid letters.
