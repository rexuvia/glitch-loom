## Summary
Data Dervish delivers the best balance of feasibility and value. Rhythm River and Gravity Garden provide standout spectacle but need careful scope control. Gemini’s set leans toward polished, familiar experiences; Grok’s offers bigger conceptual swings. Prioritize ideas that marry expressive visuals with clear daily utility (Data Dervish, Luminous Logic Labyrinth) while keeping one high-wow moonshot like Gravity Garden if resources permit.
