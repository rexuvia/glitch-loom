## Selection Philosophy

**Balanced Portfolio Approach:**
1. **Safe Bet (Data Dervish)**: Highest feasibility + value for reliable delivery
2. **Innovation Leaders (Rhythm River, Glitch Loom)**: Fresh concepts with manageable risk
3. **Proven Genre (Luminous Logic)**: Polished execution in established category
4. **Moonshot (Gravity Garden)**: High-risk, high-reward spectacle piece

**Why These 5:**
- Cover spectrum from safe→ambitious
- Diverse interaction models (creative tools, puzzles, data viz, physics sandbox)
- Strong expansion paths for all
- Balance novelty with execution realism
- Address both casual users and power users

**Notable Exclusions:**
- **Resonance Crystallizer**: Despite 9.0 wow factor, 4.5 feasibility is too risky (lowest F score overall)
- **Temporal Sandscape**: Performance concerns (5.5 F) without enough differentiation from Gravity Garden
- **Aurora Emotion Atlas**: Too familiar (6.0 U) despite solid scores
- **ChromaFlow Canvas**: Lowest uniqueness (5.5), saturated market

## Final Recommendation

**Proceed with development in this priority order:**

1. **Rhythm River** — Best overall, highest expansion potential
2. **Data Dervish** — Lowest risk, fastest to market
3. **Glitch Loom** — Most original, strong community potential
4. **Luminous Logic Labyrinth** — Reliable engagement, sustainable content

This portfolio balances innovation with pragmatism while ensuring multiple paths to success.
