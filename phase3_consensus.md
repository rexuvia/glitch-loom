## Top 5 Ideas

1. **Data Dervish** (Avg 38.3)
   - **Why:** Rated highest for user value and feasibility while still unique. Critics aligned that turning data into choreography has clear practical applications (analytics, storytelling) without overwhelming technical risk.
   - **Key Strengths:** Realistic scope (Vue+D3/Canvas), strong expansion hooks (choreography modes, music generation), daily utility for analysts/teams.

2. **Rhythm River** (Avg 38.3)
   - **Why:** Ties for top average with Data Dervish but carries slightly higher technical complexity (fluid sims). Critics saw exceptional creativity merging music composition with terrain editing.
   - **Key Strengths:** Distinctive creation mechanic, rich expansion with liquid types/weather, high wow factor for sound + visuals.

3. **Luminous Logic Labyrinth** (Avg 37.7)
   - **Why:** Balanced scores across all criteria; seen as the most buildable puzzle concept with strong replayability through content packs.
   - **Key Strengths:** Fresh take on logic puzzles, manageable tech stack (Vue + SVG), clear monetization/expansion via puzzle editor and community levels.

4. **Resonance Crystallizer** (Avg 37.7)
   - **Why:** Highest wow factor; although feasibility is lower, the judges valued its potential as a flagship experience if scoped carefully.
   - **Key Strengths:** Audio-to-crystal pipeline enables unique artifacts, immersive visuals; long-term growth via mineral types and collaborations.

5. **Glitch Loom** (Avg 37.3)
   - **Why:** Judges praised its originality and creative community potential. While niche, the technique is technically manageable compared to other high-wow concepts.
   - **Key Strengths:** Hybrid of glitch art and tactile crafting, collaborative weaving sessions, exportable outcomes for social sharing.

### Honorable Mentions
- **Gravity Garden** (Avg 37.0): Stunning spectacle; performance risk on mobile kept it out of top five.
- **Sonic Spectrum Weaver** (Avg 37.0): Solid audio viz concept but less differentiated versus the leaders.
- **Temporal Sandscape** (Avg 36.0): Loved for originality, but particle physics cost and messaging complexity are concerns.
