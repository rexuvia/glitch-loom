## Key Insights from Critics

### Grok's Observations:
- **Rhythm Weaver (Kimi)** stands out for blending geometry, particles, and audio in a novel way
- **Neural Garden** offers fresh take on neural networks as art, fits Rexuvia aesthetic well
- **Quantum Maze** is genuinely innovative but technically challenging (feasibility: 6/10)
- **Type Resonance** has niche appeal, may clash with minimal aesthetic
- **Data Constellations** feels complex for single HTML file, may not fit mobile-first

### Gemini's Observations:
- **Rhythm Weaver (Grok)** empowers non-musicians with procedural generation
- **Neural Garden** makes AI tangible and beautiful rather than abstract math
- **Quantum Maze** introduces genuine uncertainty to puzzle genre
- **Data Constellations** scored lowest - 3D may be too complex for constraints
- All rhythm/music apps scored well for user value and expansion potential

### Consensus Themes:
1. **Rhythm/Music apps are strong** - Multiple high-scoring variations
2. **Neural/AI art is promising** - Neural Garden consistently top-rated
3. **Quantum mechanics puzzles are innovative** - But may be technically challenging
4. **3D/data viz is risky** - Data Constellations scored lowest
5. **Rexuvia aesthetic fit matters** - Dark, neon, minimal considerations affect scores
