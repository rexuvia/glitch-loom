**Ode to Triangles**

Three points connect, a shape so true,
A polygon with angles few.
The simplest form that space allows,
A triad that the mind endows.

With sides that sum to one-eighty degrees,
And vertices that catch the breeze.
Equilateral, isosceles, or scalene,
The most elegant shape ever seen.

In bridges strong and pyramids tall,
In music's notes that rise and fall.
A slice of pie, a mountain's peak,
The silent strength that structures seek.

So celebrate this shape so fine,
A geometric design divine.
Three lines that meet, three corners bright,
A triangle, a perfect light.
